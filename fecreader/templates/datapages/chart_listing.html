Chart listing

This is a listing of available charts, which are embeddable on other sites or blogs. These charts update with new numbers each week. The "blog" versions of the chart are 650px wide; the "feature" versions of them are 870px wide. Both are about 500 px high. To embed these, use slightly larger dimensions. For example:
<iframe src="http://realtime.influenceexplorer.com/charts/contributions/0/blog/" width="660" height="520" frameborder="0" scrolling="no"> </iframe>
Or:
<iframe src="http://realtime.influenceexplorer.com/charts/contributions-cumulative/0/feature/" width="880" height="520" frameborder="0" scrolling="no"> </iframe>
The charts do not work on very old web browsers; Internet Explorer 10 or higher is required to view them.
